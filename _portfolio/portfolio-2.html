At the CGCA-02 Conference 2025, we presented our latest research on sustainable urban development. Our team showcased innovative strategies for integrating green spaces into city planning, emphasizing the importance of community involvement and ecological balance. The presentation included case studies from various cities that have successfully implemented these strategies, highlighting the positive impact on residents' quality of life and environmental health.
