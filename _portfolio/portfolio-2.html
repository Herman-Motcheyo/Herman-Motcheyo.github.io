Location: Yaounde, Cameroon
Date: April 08-11, 2025
Event: International Conference on Ceramics and Geomaterials in Central Africa

Presentation Details

At the CGCA-02 Conference 2025, we presented our research on machine learning applications for sustainable materials optimization. Our work demonstrates how artificial intelligence and data science techniques can be leveraged to advance ceramic materials research and promote sustainable development in Central Africa.

The presentation showcased innovative approaches combining materials science with machine learning algorithms to optimize ceramic properties, reduce environmental impact, and enhance the valorization of local geomaterials. This interdisciplinary work highlights the potential of AI-driven methodologies in addressing challenges in materials engineering and sustainable development.

Conference Impact

This conference brought together researchers, academics, and industry professionals from across Central Africa and beyond to discuss advances in ceramics, geomaterials, and their applications in construction, environmental sustainability, and technological innovation. The event emphasized the importance of valorizing local materials and fostering scientific collaboration within the African continent.
